SERVICES

Document And Text Services

Our team of experts are adept at document translation, multilingual content writing, transcription, machine translation post-editing (MTPE), proofreading, editing, terminology management, desktop publishing services (DTP) and typesetting. Our team of experienced and qualified translators offer accurate and reliable translations and are equipped to handle even the most complex translation projects. Our quality assurance process ensures that each translation undergoes rigorous proofreading and editing before delivery, ensuring that the final product is error-free and meets our high standards.

Multimedia Services

We offer a variety of services to help you communicate effectively through multimedia. KAD Translations provides outstanding services in intralingual subtitling, interlingual subtitling, subtitling for the deaf and hard of hearing (SDHH), audio translations and voiceovers. We additionally help you improve your media accessibility. We offer audiovisual translation services for a wide range of product types such as marketing videos, YouTube videos, interviews, and feature films among others. Our team works closely with you to understand your specific needs and goals, and provides customised solutions that meet your unique requirements.

Web, Mobile And Software Services

Expand your reach with our website translation, software localization, mobile content and app translation, international SEO, and game localization services. Connect with your clients at various touchpoints to improve their overall experience. This service ensures that your website or software content is adapted to meet the cultural and linguistic needs of your target audience and all content is optimised across languages for search engines. We work with you to translate and localise all user interface elements, multimedia elements and content, including text and images, to create a seamless and engaging user experience.
